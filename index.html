John Schrom, MPH, FAMIA

I'm an epidemiologist and informaticist working on infectious diseases and global health at UCSF. I primarily work on SEARCH (HIV treatment and prevention in Kenya and Uganda) and Unidos en Salud (COVID-19 testing and vaccination in the Mission district of San Francisco). In my free time, I enjoy volunteering with marine mammals and getting outside and away from computers. I'm always happy to connect via email at john@schrom.org.
