Hello, I'm John Schrom. I'm an epidemiologist and informaticist working on infectious diseases and global health at University of California, San Francisco. I primarily work on SEARCH (HIV treatment and prevention in Kenya and Uganda) and Unidos en Salud (COVID-19 testing and vaccination in the Mission district of San Francisco). I'm always happy to connect via email at john@schrom.org.
